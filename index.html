Frequently Ask Questions

How much you charge for a room in the hostel?

Different prices depending on the room.

Where are you located?

378 B Block, Faisal Town, Lahore. Opposite Fast University Gate No.2

Is there a deposit required?

The hostel does require a security deposit of 1 month. You get that back when you leave.

Is there any space for luggage?

Yes, we give cupboards for storage.

What about security?

We have CCTV Cameras installed around the hostel and everything is fed to a storage device.
